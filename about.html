Meet the Founder

Alicia Childers

Founder & Chief Strategy Officer

Alicia is a marketing executive with more than 18 years of leadership across enterprise, growth-stage, and startup companies. She has scaled marketing and revenue at CDK Global, SmileDirectClub, and Epsilon, directed more than $200 million in media investment, and helped deliver $1 billion in total shareholder returns.

At CDK Global she grew the advertising business from $40M to $170M and her team from 14 to more than 140. At SmileDirectClub she led brand and acquisition through $9B pre-IPO hypergrowth. At Epsilon she ran a $200M vertical and rebuilt trust with its largest account.

She founded AIgnite Insights to bring that enterprise-grade leadership to growing businesses, with a sharp focus on the shift everyone is racing to catch: getting found and recommended by AI. She blends data-driven marketing with AI, holds certifications in Generative AI, Applied AI, and Prompt Engineering, earned an executive degree from Harvard in Disruptive Strategy and Innovation, and works primarily in Claude.
