AI visibility, explained

What is AI visibility or Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

It is the practice of making your business readable, trusted, and quotable by AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews, so you get named and recommended when buyers ask them questions.

Why does AI visibility matter now?

Buyers are increasingly starting their search inside AI assistants instead of Google. If AI cannot read your site or does not know your business, you are invisible at the exact moment buyers are deciding who to consider.

Do you only do AI visibility?

No. We also provide AI strategy and roadmaps, done-for-you AI kits, AI-powered campaigns, and fractional CMO leadership, so you can both get found in AI and put AI to work across your business.

How fast do results come?

It varies by starting point, but many businesses begin appearing in AI answers within about 30 days of implementation, and visibility compounds from there.
