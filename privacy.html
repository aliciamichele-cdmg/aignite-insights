Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

AIgnite Insights ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using aigniteinsights.com or contacting us, you agree to this policy.

Who we are

AIgnite Insights is a marketing and AI-visibility consultancy founded by Alicia Childers, based in the Nashville, Tennessee area. You can reach us at Alicia@AigniteInsights.com or 615.807.0209.

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you submit a form on our site, use our AI Visibility Checker, request a free snapshot, or contact us, we collect the details you provide. This typically includes your name, email address, business website, and any message you send.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting and service providers may log basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you view. Our site uses Google Fonts, which may receive request data when fonts load. We do not use the site to build advertising profiles about you.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and deliver the free snapshot or other materials you request.
  • To provide, perform, and follow up on our services.
  • To send you relevant updates if you have asked to hear from us. You can opt out at any time.
  • To improve our website and offerings.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers that help us operate, and only as needed to do so. These include Formspree (which processes our website form submissions) and our website host. These providers are bound to handle your information securely. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies and analytics

Our site is intentionally lightweight and does not set marketing or tracking cookies of its own. Third-party services we use may set limited cookies necessary to function. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We keep the information you provide for as long as needed to respond to you, deliver our services, and meet legal or business requirements. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you, by emailing Alicia@AigniteInsights.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, and we will honor those rights.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to safeguard the information you share with us.

Children

Our site and services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above.
